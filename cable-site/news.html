News: October 7, 2007
FINALLY!!!!!

Cableís debut album VARIABLE SPEED DRIVE will be re-released next year via Translation Loss Records.

VSD was originally released in 1996 via Doghouse Records and has been out of print for years. The album will receive an overhaul this spring, it will be remixed & remastered @ Super 8 Studioís in Philadelphia by our good friend Mick Mullin..Please keep an eye out for more details and in the meantime, check out the Variable Speed Drive Myspace page.

More to come......Cable

 News: September 19, 2007
Welcome to the new official Cable website. Its content is still in the works, but keep checking for updates and grand opening of the first Cable online store.

2007 has been a quiet year for all the members of Cable in terms of music. Everyone has been busy with regular life shit but we did manage to release the Last Call CD/DVD on Translation Loss Records and record a song for the Eyehategod tribute CD on Emetic Records entitled For The Sick. The full track list can be viewed & the 2XCD can be purchased here.

Please keep an eye out for reissues of classic Cable t-shirt designs to be available ONLY from this site along with most of our record catalog and you never know...maybe some surprises.
