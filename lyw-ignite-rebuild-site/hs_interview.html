The Sun Rises... review from HM Magazine #105 (Jan. / Feb. 2004)
by Seth W.

Melodic hardcore either brings honest work or cliche hacking. Thankfully, Life in Your Way present their brand of hardcore in truthful light. Avoiding the trend of over processed, sparkling clean production The Sun Rises... still sounds like a hardcore album even during its more quiet times. Song structure is your standard collections of dynamic changes, spoken word parts, and gang vocals, yet it's pulled off tightly enough to avoid sounding tired and worn.
