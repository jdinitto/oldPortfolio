Wednesday, December 21st, 2005 {posted by Joshua}
We're back from the Europe tour. It was one of the "best tours ever" for LIYW. The kids there were so excited to see us play every night. A lot of sloppy dancing, crowd surfing, and circle pits. The crowds at shows seemed more positive and outgoing than here. We also saw some amazing things in history like the Colosseum in Rome. It was an all around great time. We'll be putting together pictures for the site and hopefully a few video clips. Stay tuned for that.

And now something we haven't let too many people know about yet: Ignite and Rebuild has been picked up and released by a label in Japan called FabTone Records. FabTone has had our new CD out for over a month now. We've heard the response has been great and there is a possible tour the works for LIYW to hit Japan. We will definitely let you know any news when it comes about a tour in Japan. Check out their site here.

Meanwhile, we'll be home working on material for a new album. Keep on the look out for local shows. Come out for the show in Waterbury on 12/22, as it should be an awesome time. Merry Christmas and stay warm!

joshua LIYW
